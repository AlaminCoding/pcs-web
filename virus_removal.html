Virus Removal

Clean and Secure Your Website

Is your PC slowing down? Can't get rid of annoying pop-ups? Computer crashes randomly? It could mean you are affected by malware, which is short for Malicious software. Virus is one kind of malware. But there are other kinds of malware, which simply destroys data and a seriously harmful element to operating systems.

We help you

  • Remove malware from PC
  • Remove adware from MAC
  • Clean up viruses on phone
  • Remove spyware & ransomware

Process of Cleaning

We check for symptoms of a malware infection, enter safe mode and back up your files except the program files, as those might be infected. Afterwards, we run the scan, detect malwares and remove them. Finally, the old files are restored and the malware cleaning process is completed.
